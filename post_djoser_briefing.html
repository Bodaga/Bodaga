APA DOSSIER – SUBJECT: "WILHELM"

APA FILE: APA INTERNAL BRIEFING

Subject: Post-Djoser Incident Debrief
Division: Arcane Threat Assessment Unit
Clearance Level: Yellow 3
Date: D+3 Post-Event
Compiled by: Agent M. Keene, Special Analysis Division

Executive Summary:

Following the unauthorized intrusion at the Djoser Locus Site, the primary four anomalous individuals referred to as “STONEHENGE” (see Ref: STONEHENGE-01) are confirmed to have entered the pyramid, bypassed Order of the Veil surveillance, and engaged in combat with an embedded Sons of Aether detachment led by now-deceased operative Luther Morrigan.

Analysis suggests that despite prior hostile engagement with APA operatives and consistent evasion of surveillance, the group actively opposed the SoA at the Locus and repaired the ritual circle, counter to the SoA's destabilization directive.

This action has disrupted standard threat alignment models. Their actions cannot be reliably categorized under any currently monitored hostile faction.

Key Findings:

  • Point of Entry: Stonehenge arrived on-site under SoA escort. [REDACTED] confirmed initial cooperation with Luther Morrigan.
  • Separation Event: Stonehenge diverged from SoA escort during the tomb access phase. [REDACTED] states “they refused to share findings.”
  • Engagement Timeline:
    • Magical activity surge recorded at 19:42 local time.
    • Internal monitoring registered brief destabilization of the veil, followed by immediate stabilization.
    • Spectral aurora noted over the site by Order observers.
    • Luther Morrigan yet to be recovered.
    • Ritual Circle yet to be accessed.
  • Survivors: Two SoA operatives fled and were apprehended by APA agents.
    • Both testified to:
      • Hostile engagement with the Stonehenge.
      • Circle was actively being interacted with by Stonehenge to unknown ends.

Subject Profiles (Updated):

  • Wilhelm: Wizard-class anomaly. Unknown origin.
  • Apollo: Sorcerer-class anomaly. Suspected link to ancestral magical lineage.
  • Bud E. Holly: Druidic class anomaly. History of anti-institutional rhetoric.
  • Shirley Nesbitt: Paladin-class anomaly. Claims Christian divine conduit status. Known to inspire cult-like loyalty among civilians. Unclear if contact is genuine or anomalous mimicry.

Assessment:

“They don’t work for the Sons, they fight us, and they don’t share Order ideology either. That means they’re working toward something else entirely — either they don’t trust anyone, or they’re trying to claim the circles for themselves.”

The primary hypothesis emerging is that the Stonehenge represent a self-directed faction, whose motives are not yet fully understood. Their protection of the ritual structure is anomalous and potentially strategic — perhaps to control or monopolize access to ambient magical build-up for their own ends.

Secondary hypotheses include:

  • Third-party agents from the other side acting to exert control over our world.
  • Order agents gone off-the-books.

MEASURES:

  • Immediate reclassification of the Stonehenge as High-Priority Unaligned Magical Threats.
  • Deploy Surveillance Teams to [REDACTED].
  • Recover access to Djoser site.

Addendum:

The circle remains intact. Whether by choice or chance, they preserved the veil. The question is why.
