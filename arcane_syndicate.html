APA DOSSIER – SUBJECT: ARCANE SYNDICATE

CODE DESIGNATION: BLACKWATER

STATUS: HIGH PRIORITY MONITORING

OVERVIEW:

Covert network of spellcasters, artificers, and magical traffickers primarily operating across Europe, North Africa, and parts of the Middle East. Suspected front operations in antique arms trading, academic institutions, and restoration industries. Regular contact with at least one known demonic entity (see: INTERLOCUTOR FENRAX).

ARCANE CLASSIFICATION:

Highly varied. Known associates range from Class III to Class IX arcana. Reports of temporal incantation (prohibited class) confirmed.

NOTES:

  • Core leadership structure unknown. Suspected decentralised command.
  • Confirmed to have infiltrated several magical supply chains.
  • Working hypothesis: network aims to reintroduce widespread magic through black-market deployment and public veils breach.
  • Possible ties to Sons of Aether via financial intermediaries. See ongoing audits.

THREAT ASSESSMENT:

EXTREME. Organization poses a credible threat to veil integrity. Members exhibit high loyalty and operate with tactical intelligence. Containment or elimination of nodes should be considered top priority.
