ACTIVE THREAT DOSSIER – ARCANE SYNDICATE

ENTITY: Arcane Syndicate (Designation: A-S)

Compiled by: APA Intelligence & Analysis Division
Clearance Level: T-4 or above
Last Updated: [Today’s Date]

SUMMARY:

The Arcane Syndicate (A-S) is a loosely coordinated network of magical black market operators, rogue mages, and privateers. Each Ring is controlled by a "Ringmaster" whose operational focus and goals vary. No central command structure has been confirmed. Inter-Ring cooperation is rare and opportunistic.

KNOWN RINGMASTERS:

Note: Intelligence remains fragmentary. Relations with one Ringmaster offer no guarantees regarding others.
  • "Thorn" – Western Europe. Operates in Romanian and Italian arcane black markets.
  • Dr. Zhen Rao – South China. Expelled academic focused on biological arcana and fleshcrafting.
  • "Mistress Kerosene" – Southeast Asia. Specializes in fire enchantments. Known former SoA associate.
  • "Nails" – Central US. Supplier of anti-scrying tech. Evaded APA capture 7 times.
  • "Goldeye" – Southern Africa. Linked to weapons trade and Tier 1 Upington breach.

CURRENT INTELLIGENCE OBJECTIVES:

  • Assess inter-Ring communications and possible structural hierarchy.
  • Track Syndicate–SoA collaborations and shared assets.
  • Capture and reverse-engineer “Black Wax Seal” anti-scrying devices.
  • Interrogate clients and proxies for Ringmaster origin data.

RELATED INCIDENTS:

  • Budapest Transit Node Explosion (2008): Failed teleportation portal. 11 civilian deaths. Syndicate materials confirmed on-site.
  • Russian Whisper Plague (2018): Curse transmitted via sound, allegedly brokered by A-S for SoA deployment. Investigation ongoing.

APA RISK PROFILE:

  • Threat Tier: Variable (T2–T5 by Ring)
  • Stability: Low
  • Infiltration Risk: Moderate–High
  • Public Exposure Risk: Medium
  • Affiliations: SoA (transactional), private collectors, OotV exiles

RECOMMENDED PROTOCOLS:

  • Assume no Ring-to-Ring alignment. Treat each cell independently.
  • Engage regional authorities via secure, firewalled communication only.
  • Route all A-S materials to Arcane Containment for triage and classification.
  • Capture of Ringmasters authorized when tactical conditions permit.
