ORGANIZATIONAL TRAITS

  • Field operatives referred to as “Adherents” – used as APA intermediaries or messengers.
  • Operations are minimalistic; emphasis on secrecy and ritual containment.

APA POSITION

  • Does not obstruct APA operations; often allows APA to handle field escalations.

INCIDENT REFERENCES

THREAT ASSESSMENT:

Low-visibility, high-persistence actor. Tolerated due to cooperative stance and pre-emptive containment capability.

Long-term threat potential: Moderate. Monitor closely for shifts in doctrine or leadership emergence.
